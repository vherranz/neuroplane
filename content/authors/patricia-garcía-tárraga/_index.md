Patricia García Tárraga is the Lab Manager of the NeuroPlaNe Lab. She holds a Higher Technician degree in Pathological Anatomy and has nearly 20 years of experience in sample preparation and processing. Her expertise includes immunohistochemistry, immunogold labeling, and electron microscopy, playing a central role in ensuring the quality and reliability of the lab’s ultrastructural and histological analyses.
